Open Source. Open culture.

Mechanical Ink supports, contributes to, and creates open-source software and products in the civic, educational, and ethical tech space. We believe in the transformative capacity of knowledge sharing in enabling individuals and groups to create opportunities for themselves and others.

We are an Open Startup

Mechanical Ink was founded on the following core principles:

  • Freedom of access to information.
  • Access to the world wide web is a human right that needs to be protected.
  • Owning your content through decentralization and syndication through open APIs.
  • The transformative power of open source and our responsibility to ensure the ecosystem is sustainable.
  • Promote and support the use and evolution of open web standards.
  • Your data is yours to do with as you choose, not a form of currency to be abused by large corporations.
  • Embrace a diverse culture through a remote-first work policy.
